Log your spending and incomes directly from your website under wordpress. You will be able to follow your spending whereever you are.

== Description ==

Spending tracker will allow you to track your expenses and incomes. You will also be able to prepare your account state.

The supplier and the project functionnality will allow you to order your spending correctly and to know where you have spend more or less money.
It will also allow you to know the real cost of your projects.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/spending-tracker` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Access main dashboard and start adding your expenses and incomes
